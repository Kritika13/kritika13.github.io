Yum-althy A Branding Project

Yum-althy launched by Priyanka , who is a home chef , is a food blog on instagram. Yum-althy presents an array of flavoursome food including kebabs , burgers, cake, muffins, smoothie, fresh from home, to fulfil all the exotic cravings, but in a healthy way!! Yumalthy practices stringent hygienic protocols while cooking, packing & delivering. Ensuring the products reach the customers safely. Sending across those smiles in form of our Good Food language is all what we look forward to!
